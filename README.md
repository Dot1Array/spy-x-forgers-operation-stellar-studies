🎮 GAMEPLAY PREMISE

At the beginning of the game, Anya is given an assignment by her adviser Mr. Henderson, who
informs Anya that she has an assignment that requires her to create a photo collage of memories
spent with peers, which should properly demonstrate the elegance of Eden Academy's students
in and around their respective communities. Anya is also reminded of an upcoming set of
assessments for students that will properly asses their mathematical and physical skills, of which
the best overall performing students in both the assessments and the project will be given a Stella Star.

Anya will have exactly 40 days to accomplish all of these tasks, which are to prepare and study
for the Math and Physical Assessments by being tutored by her parents Loid Forger, and Yor
Forger respectively. At the same time, she'll have to plan outings (called Ootings by Anya) and
spend time with her family while bonding with her peers during school days.
*****************************************************************************************************************************
GAMEPLAY OBJECTIVES

Within the span of 40 days, players must use their time wisely in helping Anya bond with her peers
while studying for Math and training for her Physical Education assessments. While studying, she
should bond with her peers to properly unlock places where she can have her outings. During her
outings, players should guide her to take the Perfect Photo that she can add to her photo collage.
*****************************************************************************************************************************
STARTING VALUES

Anya starts the game with the following stats:
+ 3 AP = AP (Activity Points) determine how much Energy Anya has to do certain activities available to her.
More on this in later sections.
+ 0 BP for everybody (Desmond, Becky, Mr. Henderson, Bond) = BP (Bond Points) refer to her Bond Level with the
four aforementioned individuals. She can raise these values through Bond Activities, which are discussed in Section VI.
+ Math Level 1 = This determines how proficient she is in Math, and how the Study Activity Minigame will play out.
She earns Levels based on how she performs in this mini game.
+ PE Level 1 = This determines how proficient she is in Physical Education, and how the PE Activity Minigame will play out.
She earns Levels based on how she performs in this mini game.
+ 5 Camera Rolls = This determines the number of attempts she has at taking photos during her Outing Days.
This is an important factor for the Memorable Photo minigame.
*****************************************************************************************************************************
NON-OUTING DAYS

On non-outing days, you choose activities for morning and afternoon.
Most actions cost 1 AP, except resting. Each function requires inputs within a specific range:
Math Minigame: Answer math questions. Invalid answers repeat.
PE Minigame: Match Yor's direction using the correct key. Wrong or invalid inputs repeat.
Photo Minigame: Guess a number 1-1000. Anything outside this range shows an error.
Venue Selection: Choose from 1-7; locked or out-of-range choices show an error.
Non-Outing Activities (1-4): Options 1-3 need AP; option 4 is free. Invalid choices repeat.
Errors always make the game ask again until the player enters a valid input.
*****************************************************************************************************************************
OUTING DAYS

On outing days, the player visits the chosen venue and plays through a morning and afternoon sequence. Some actions cost AP, and the game checks the following:
Morning Outing: Choose between available options (usually 1-2). If you don't have enough AP, the game shows an error and repeats the question.
Activities & Bonding: Actions may increase photo ratings or bond levels depending on choices.
Input Validation:
Only valid options (usually 1-2) are accepted.
Any number outside the given range prints an error and asks again.
Venue Effects: Each venue has randomized values that affect the best photo result.
Outing days always guide the player through dialogue, choices, and results, and the game does not move forward until valid input is given.
*****************************************************************************************************************************
GAMEPLAY MECHANICS

Each activity that Anya performs costs 1 AP, including: Bond Activities and Mini-games
If Anya does not have enough AP, she cannot select an activity that requires AP.
Free Actions (Do NOT cost AP):
Proceed Home (Regular School Day - Morning at Eden Academy)
Slack Off (Regular School Day - Afternoon at Home)
Go to Ooting! (Outing Day - Morning Preparation)
Go Home (Outing Day - Morning - Ooting Time!)
Decide Next Outing Venue (Outing Day - Evening - Family Meeting)
Daily AP Recovery:
At the end of each day, Anya automatically gains +1 AP except: on the 3rd regular school day of each 4-day cycle (no AP gained)
Cycle Reset Rule:
At the end of a 4-day cycle, Anya's AP resets to 3 unless she has more than 3 AP saved (e.g., she has 5 AP).
In that case, she keeps her higher AP amount.
*****************************************************************************************************************************
BOND ACTIVITIES

Anya can perform Bond Activities with four characters: Damian, Becky, Mr. Henderson, and Bond.
Each character starts with 0 Bond Points (BP), and Anya can earn up to 5 BP per character to reach their maximum bond level.

Performing a Bond Activity:
Costs 1 AP
Advances the day/phase automatically
After bonding with Damian, Becky, or Mr. Henderson, Anya goes home
After bonding with Bond, the day ends

When bonding, Anya can earn 1 BP depending on her stats:
        Damian Desmond:
        Level 1 PE = 0% chance
        Level 2 PE = 25%
        Level 3 PE = 50%
        MAX PE = 75%
        Becky Blackbell:
        Always 75% chance, regardless of stats
        Mr. Henderson:
        Level 1 Math = 0%
        Level 2 Math = 50%
        Level 3 Math = 75%
        MAX Math = 100%
        Bond Forger:
        Always 100% chance to gain 1 BP
*****************************************************************************************************************************
MINIGAMES

When Anya completes a minigame, she earns EXP based on her performance: 60% correct grants 1 EXP, 80% correct grants 2 EXP,
and 100% correct grants 3 EXP with a 50% chance of not consuming an AP point and awarding an additional 1 AP.
The EXP required to level up increases with each level: 5 EXP for Level 1, 10 EXP for Level 2, and 15 EXP for Level 3,
and EXP resets to 0 upon leveling up. At Level MAX, Anya no longer gains EXP, but achieving 100% still gives a 60% chance
of not using an AP point and grants 2 additional AP. Regardless of performance, playing a minigame ends the day and
progresses the game to the next day.
*****************************************************************************************************************************
MATH MINIGAME

The Study Math with Loid minigame allows the player to help Anya improve her mathematical skills by answering randomly
generated equations. In each round, two integers and an operator are displayed, and Anya must provide the correct answer.
The difficulty and type of questions depend on AnyaΓÇÖs Math Level: Level 1 features addition and subtraction with numbers
from 1 to 999 (5 questions); Level 2 adds multiplication with numbers from 1 to 20 and uses values up to 9999 (10 questions);
Level 3 expands multiplication to numbers up to 50 while keeping addition and subtraction with numbers up to 9999
(10 questions); Level MAX has the same range and operations as Level 3.
*****************************************************************************************************************************
PHYSICAL EDUCATION MINIGAME

Train Physical Activity with Yor helps Anya improve her PE skills by testing her reaction time.
Yor gives prompts, and Anya must input the correct corresponding action. The prompts vary by PE Level:
PE Level 1: 10 random prompts consisting of Up! = 'w'/'W', Down! = 's'/'S', Left! = 'a'/'A', Right! = 'd'/'D'.
PE Level 2: 10 random prompts including Level 1 prompts plus North! = '8', South! = '2', East! = '6', West! = '4'.
PE Level 3: 10 random prompts including all Level 2 prompts plus UP AHEAD! = 'W', BEHIND YOU! = 'S', TO THE LEFT! = 'A',
TO THE RIGHT! = 'D', and diagonals Northwest! = '7', Northeast! = '9', Southwest! = '1', Southeast! = '3'.
PE Level MAX: Identical to Level 3, with all above prompts included.
Anya must provide the correct input for each prompt to successfully complete the minigame.
*****************************************************************************************************************************
MEMORABLE PHOTO MINIGAME

The ΓÇ£Memorable PhotoΓÇ¥ minigame allows Anya to collect memorable photos during outings with Loid and Yor.
In this minigame, Anya has 5 rolls to guess a number between 1 and 1000, which is randomly generated for
each venue at the start of the playthrough and remains the same for that venue throughout the game.
Her photo rating depends on how close her guess is to the venue's magical number:
NOT GOOD: Default rating for venues where the minigame hasn't been played or guesses are far from the magical number.
OKAY: Guess is within 100 points of the magical number.
VERY GOOD: Guess is within 10 points of the magical number.
PERFECT: Guess matches the magical number exactly.
Unlike other minigames, Anya can re-attempt the Memorable Photo minigame as long as she has remaining AP.

PLAYER STATISTICS

As the players play through the game, they are able to view the Stats that the player has obtained
so far, ranging from the Levels of Anya, her previous performances in the photos, and her bond levels.

ENDINGS

Once the 40 days have elapsed, Anya's performance will lead her to the game's ending.
Perfect Ending: Anya succeeds in submitting her project and ends up with near-top ranks in the
exams. As a result, she is awarded the Stella Star, with Mr. Henderson praising her for her
performance being ΓÇ£Very ElegantΓÇ¥. This ending is achievable by obtaining a PERFECT rating in ALL memorable photos for ALL venues.
Neutral Ending: Anya submits her project, but there are better projects that have been submitted.
However, she passes her exams, but does not earn the Stella Star. This ending is achieved by
obtaining a PERFECT memorable photo rating in at least 3 venues and maxing out her Math and PE levels.
Bad Ending: Anya submits her project, but it is incomplete. Likewise, she does not pass her
exams and, to Loid's dismay, Anya gets a Tonitrus bolt for her performance. This ending is
obtained if less than 3 PERFECT memorable photo ratings are obtained or her PE and MATH levels are not maxed out.
